In a significant regulatory move, the White House has moved to restrict Anthropic's AI model, 'Fable', following security research conducted by Amazon. This highlights growing concerns over AI safety and the necessity of government oversight regarding powerful AI models.

### AI Coding Agents Struggle with Critical Bug Detection
New research indicates a critical limitation in current AI coding agents: while they are highly effective at locating the correct files for a task, they frequently miss critical bug lines during the code review process. This finding underscores the need for more robust verification mechanisms in automated development workflows.
