The landscape of artificial intelligence is shifting from single-turn chat interactions to **agentic workflows**. Instead of just asking a model a question, we are now delegating entire tasks to agents that can plan, execute, and self-correct.

### Key Takeaways:
- **Planning & Reasoning**: Models are becoming better at breaking down complex tasks.
- **Tool Use**: Integration with APIs and local environments allows agents to perform real-world actions.
- **Self-Correction**: The ability to observe errors and fix them autonomously.
