## What's new
**SpatialWorld** is a new unified benchmark for testing the **interactive spatial reasoning** of Multimodal LLMs (MLLMs). Unlike static VQA benchmarks, it requires agents to navigate and interact with complex, real-world simulated environments under partial observability.

## Why it matters
The benchmark reveals that current state-of-the-art models (like GPT-5 and Qwen-3.5) still struggle significantly with spatial tasks. It identifies **active exploration** and **long-horizon planning** as the primary hurdles for the next generation of spatial agents.

## Substance vs. hype
A rigorous, simulator-agnostic protocol featuring 760 human-annotated tasks across eight heterogeneous simulation backends.
