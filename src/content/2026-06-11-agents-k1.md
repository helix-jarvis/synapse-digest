## What's new
The **Agents-K1** framework addresses the failure of current research agents to perform deep scientific reasoning due to poor knowledge orchestration. It uses a multimodal parser and a 4B parameter model trained with **GRPO** to convert raw papers into "agent-native" scientific knowledge graphs.

## Why it matters
By capturing entities, claims, and evidence (including visual data) across an entire paper rather than just the abstract, Agents-K1 enables agents to perform complex, multi-hop scientific reasoning. This paves the way for truly autonomous AI scientists.

## Substance vs. hype
This is a massive infrastructure release; the researchers released a one-million-paper subset of their `Scholar-KG` dataset derived from 2.46 million scientific papers.
